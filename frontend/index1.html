About RINL

Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited
Visakhapatnam Steel Plant

RINL, the corporate entity of Visakhapatnam Steel Plant (VSP), is a Navratna Central Public Sector Enterprise under the Ministry of Steel, Government of India. Established in 1982 and commissioned in 1992, VSP is India's first shore-based integrated steel plant.

This Contractor Wage Management System is built to streamline payroll processing for contractors across all worker categories — ensuring timely, accurate, and compliant wage disbursements as per government norms.

System Features

What the Wage System Does

👷
Worker Registration
Register and manage contractor workers across all four categories under each contractor firm.
📅
Attendance Tracking
Daily attendance with present, absent, half-day, and overtime hours for accurate computation.
💰
Wage Computation
Auto-calculate gross wages, PF, ESI, overtime, and net payable as per Minimum Wages Act.
📄
Wage Sheet Generation
Contractor-wise and category-wise monthly wage sheets ready for approval and printing.
🛡️
Role-Based Access
Supervisors, HR managers, and admins each have their own secure access levels.
📈
Analytics & Reports
Monthly summaries, contractor-wise payroll reports and workforce category dashboards.
