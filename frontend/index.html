About RINL

Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited
Visakhapatnam Steel Plant

RINL, the corporate entity of Visakhapatnam Steel Plant (VSP), is a Navratna Central Public Sector Enterprise under the Ministry of Steel, Government of India. Established in 1982 and commissioned in 1992, VSP is India's first shore-based integrated steel plant.

This Contractor Wage Management System is built to streamline payroll processing for contractors across all worker categories - ensuring timely, accurate, and compliant wage disbursements as per government norms.

By digitizing the entire wage lifecycle, RINL aims to increase transparency, reduce administrative overhead, and foster a healthier, more productive relationship with its vast contractor workforce. It serves as a unified portal bridging the gap between contractors, workers, and RINL management.

System Features

What the Wage System Does

Worker Registration
Register and rigorously manage contractor workers across all four specialized categories under each contractor firm with full profile details.
Attendance Tracking
Log daily attendance seamlessly with precise tracking for present, absent, half-day, and overtime hours for highly accurate computation.
Wage Computation
Automatically calculate gross wages, provident fund (PF), ESI, overtime rates, and net payable strictly as per the Minimum Wages Act.
Wage Sheet Generation
Generate detailed contractor-wise and category-wise monthly wage sheets ready for swift management approval and hard-copy printing.
Role-Based Access
Supervisors, HR managers, and system administrators each have their own customized, secure access levels preventing unauthorized data access.
Analytics & Reports
Access detailed monthly summaries, comprehensive contractor-wise payroll reports, and high-level workforce category performance dashboards.
