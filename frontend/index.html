About

Worker-focused portal for wage transparency

This system is designed as a polished internship demo for managing daily wage workers under contractors. It focuses on authentication, approval, attendance, and wage calculation.

Aadhaar OTP and real SMS OTP are kept as future enhancements to avoid paid or restricted APIs during the demo stage.
